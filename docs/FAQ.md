# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is a notary public?
A notary public is a person authorized by the state to witness and authenticate the signing of important documents.

## What services do notary publics offer?
Notary publics typically offer services such as:  
- Acknowledgments  
- Jurats  
- Oaths and affirmations  
- Signature witnessing  
- Copies of documents  
- Travel notarizations  

## How do I know if I need a notary?
You typically need a notary for documents that require legal verification of signatures, such as real estate transactions, wills, and power of attorney documents.

## What is an ordained service?
Ordained services refer to ceremonies or functions conducted by an ordained individual, such as weddings, baptisms, or other religious ceremonies.
